# dJulkalendern

The dJulkalendern committee is responsible for creating dJulkalendern every year. dJulkalendern 
is a calendar based CTF-competition that runs from start of december to christmas eve. Challenges
are released on weekdays at 12:15. 

Each window that are released are cryptic puzzles, hacking and other computer science related
things and your task is to solve as many of them, and as fast, as possible! The calendar has an 
intriguing story that unfolds as you solve each window. 

The competition is held on our website [https://djulkalendern.se](https://djulkalendern.se). 

There is also a [discord server](https://dsekt.se/djul) that you can join to get updates
before and during the calendar.
