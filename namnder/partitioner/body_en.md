# Partitions
Partitions are smaller groups within the CS-chapter that arrange events and activities based on a common area of ​​interest.

If you want to start a partition to gather people who share one of your interests, contact the board via [drek@datasektionen.se](mailto:drek@datasektionen.se).

If you have any questions about partitions, you may refer to the [PM for Partitions](https://styrdokument.datasektionen.se/pm/pm_partitioner) (only available in Swedish for now) or contact the board via [drek@datasektionen.se](mailto:drek@datasektionen.se).
